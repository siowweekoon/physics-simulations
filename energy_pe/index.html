📌 Key Ideas

Pump = Battery
Actively pushes water (charge) around the loop. It does not rely on gravity — it creates the pressure (voltage) that drives flow.

Height = Voltage
The higher the pump lifts the water, the greater the gravitational PE — just like higher voltage = greater electrical PE.

Flow Rate = Current
More water passing the wheel per second = more current. Controlled by both pump power and pipe width.

Water Wheel = Resistor / Load
Converts the water's PE into useful work (spin). A resistor converts electrical energy into heat & light.
